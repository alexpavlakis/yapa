2020

Updated April 13, 2020.

Who will win the electoral college?

Biden is projected to win between 238 and 372 electoral college votes. Trump is projected to win between 166 and 299 electoral college votes. In 25000 simulations of the presidential election from the perspective of April 13, 2020, Biden wins 81% and Trump wins 18%.

Who will win the popular vote?

Biden is projected to win between 46% and 54% of the national popular vote. Trump is projected to win between 41% and 49% of the national popular vote. In 25000 simulations of the popular vote from the perspective of April 13, 2020, Biden wins 83% and Trump wins 17%.
