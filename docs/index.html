Who will win the presidency?

Current snapshot

Biden is projected to win between 215 - 379 electoral college votes. Trump is projected to win between 152 - 316 electoral college votes. In 25000 simulations of the presidential election from the perspective of March 28th, 2020, Biden wins 74% and Trump wins 26%.

Who will win each state?

Overview

Note that uncertainties at this time are due to a) close races and/or b) few polls. As more polls become available and election day nears, error bars should shrink and the range of reasonable potential outcomes should narrow.
