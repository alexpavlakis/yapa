2020

Updated April 23, 2020.

Who will win the electoral college?

Biden is projected to win between 237 and 370 electoral college votes. Trump is projected to win between 167 and 301 electoral college votes. In 25000 simulations of the presidential election from the perspective of April 23, 2020, Biden wins 80% and Trump wins 19%.

Who will win the popular vote?

Biden is projected to win between 46% and 53% of the national popular vote. Trump is projected to win between 41% and 48% of the national popular vote. In 25000 simulations of the popular vote from the perspective of April 23, 2020, Biden wins 84% and Trump wins 16%.
