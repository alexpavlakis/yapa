2020

Updated April 8, 2020.

Who will win the electoral college?

Biden is projected to win between 234 and 370 electoral college votes. Trump is projected to win between 168 and 304 electoral college votes. In 25000 simulations of the presidential election from the perspective of April 8, 2020, Biden wins 78% and Trump wins 21%.

Who will win each state?
