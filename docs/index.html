2020

Updated April 12, 2020.

Who will win the electoral college?

Biden is projected to win between 242 and 377 electoral college votes. Trump is projected to win between 161 and 295 electoral college votes. In 25000 simulations of the presidential election from the perspective of April 12, 2020, Biden wins 84% and Trump wins 16%.

Who will win the popular vote?

Biden is projected to win between 46% and 53% of the national popular vote. Trump is projected to win between 41% and 49% of the national popular vote. In 25000 simulations of the popular vote from the perspective of April 12, 2020, Biden wins 82% and Trump wins 18%.
