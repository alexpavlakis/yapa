2020 - House

Updated July 10, 2020, 116 days from election day 2020.

Who will win control of the House?

The Democrats are projected to win between 221 and 242 seats in the House. The Republicans are projected to win between 193 and 214 seats in the House. In 10000 simulations of the House elections from the perspective of July 10, 2020, the Democrats win a majority in 98% and the Republicans win a majority in 2%.
