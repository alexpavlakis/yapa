2020 - Senate

Updated July 13, 2020, 113 days from election day 2020.

Who will win control of the Senate?

The Democrats are projected to win between 47 and 57 seats in the Senate. The Republicans are projected to win between 42 and 52 seats in the Senate. In 25000 simulations of the Senate elections from the perspective of July 13, 2020, the Democrats win a majority in 74% and the Republicans win a majority in 26%. (Assuming the two independent senators, neither of whom is up for re-election, continue to caucus with the Democrats).
