2020 - Senate

Updated October 30, 2020, 4 days from election day 2020.

Who will win control of the Senate?

The Democrats are projected to win between 44 and 60 seats in the Senate. The Republicans are projected to win between 39 and 55 seats in the Senate. In 25000 simulations of the Senate elections from the perspective of October 30, 2020, the Democrats win a majority in 56% and the Republicans win a majority in 44%. (Assuming the two independent senators, neither of whom is up for re-election, continue to caucus with the Democrats).
